Our First Product: ChainBooks

ChainBooks is an advanced accounting solution powered by AI and blockchain. It aims to handle 80–90% of your routine accounting tasks, leaving you free to focus on what truly matters. Simply review the AI-generated entries and send them straight into your existing systems—Tally, Zoho, or any other software you use.

But ChainBooks is more than just accounting automation. It also comes with AI Agents to guide you through complex work. Need help filing taxes accurately? Our Tax Assistant is at your service. Want to draft legal documents, respond to notices, or manage ROC (Registrar of Companies) documents? Our Legal Drafting and Notice Reply Agents are there. Looking for a private AI chat system (Private GPT) to process your own data securely? ChainBooks offers that too. Even talking directly with your data to find insights becomes simple. With these AI Agents, compliance tasks become smoother, faster, and more reliable.
